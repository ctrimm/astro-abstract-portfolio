## SONIC_TEXTURES

A web player that physically degrades the audio quality every time you play a track. By the 100th play, it is just white noise. A commentary on the impermanence of digital media.

### TECH_STACK

*   **Audio Processing**: Real-time bit-crushing and high-pass filtering in the browser.
*   **Visuals**: Oscilloscopes drawn on HTML5 Canvas.
