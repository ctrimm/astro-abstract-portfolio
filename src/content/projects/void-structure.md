## ARCHITECTURAL_DIGITIZATION

Translating the oppressive scale of brutalist concrete structures into DOM elements. We used heavy borders, lack of spacing, and high-contrast typography to mimic the feeling of standing at the foot of a Soviet-era monument.

> "Form follows failure."

### IMPLEMENTATION

*   **Grid Systems**: A strict 12-column grid that is frequently broken and ignored.
*   **Typography**: Massive scale text that bleeds off the viewport.
