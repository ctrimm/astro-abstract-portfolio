## CONCEPT_EXECUTION

The objective was to strip away all user-friendly interfaces and present raw data streams. We utilized WebGL to render million-point datasets in real-time, creating a chaotic yet informative dashboard for the post-privacy era.

### TECHNICAL_CHALLENGES

*   **Performance Optimization**: Rendering 1M+ particles at 60fps required custom shader work.
*   **User Hostility**: Intentionally difficult navigation to force user engagement and learning.

## FINAL_OUTPUT

A mesmerizing void of information that swallows the user whole.
